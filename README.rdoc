Noodle is a web application for news researcher. It will analyze a given news topics, and return to you with the big moment of that topic. You can also customize the search, as well as the result, to fit your best interest.

portal: http://noodl.herokuapp.com/

Noodle is currently seeded with Elon Musk news from Guardian and New York Times since 2013. Keyword Analysis is powered by Text Razor and IBM Watson Alchemy API. Feel free to search anything about him.
